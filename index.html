Admission Policy

×

Eligibility Criteria

Admission is open to all students regardless of caste, creed, or religion. The minimum age for Nursery is 3 years as of April 1st of the academic year.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (Original & Photocopy)
  • Previous School's Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Recent Passport size photographs (4)
  • Aadhar Card of Student and Parents

Admission Process

Forms are available at the school office. Selection for higher classes is based on an entrance assessment followed by an interaction with the Principal.

Examination Cell

×

Evaluation System

School follow karta hai CCE (Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation) pattern. Isme Unit Tests (PT-1, PT-2), Half-Yearly aur Annual Examinations shamil hain.

Important Instructions

  • Examination Hall mein Admit Card lana compulsory hai.
  • 75% Attendance honi chahiye exam mein baithne ke liye.
  • Kisi bhi tarah ki cheating pakde jane par pariksha radd (cancel) kar di jayegi.

Result Declaration

Annual Result har saal March ke aakhri hafte mein declare kiya jata hai.

Yahan Result Dekhein: Student ERP Portal
