⚠️ Safety Protocol

  • Wear Lab Coat and Safety Goggles at all times.
  • Do not touch chemicals or wires with bare hands.
  • In case of emergency, use the Safety Shower/Eye Wash.
  • Locate Fire Extinguisher before starting experiments.

📌 General Lab Rules

  • Maintain pin-drop silence during practicals.
  • Mobile phones and bags are not allowed inside.
  • Handle all glass apparatus with extreme care.
  • Clean and dry all equipment before returning.
  • Follow Teacher's instructions strictly.
